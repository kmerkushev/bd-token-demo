Personal data resale

State and corporate censorship
Governments have the power to decide for you what you see and consume on the internet. They can control your access to certain apps and ban services that don’t conform to their political narratives. In our opinion, this is due to the fact that Google and Apple app stores don’t allow blockchain products, dApps and tokens on their platforms.

State and corporate censorship

Black Duck develops mobile operating systems that give users  the right to restrict or completely block unauthorized use of their personal data. The company believes that everyone deserves  to have complete control and freedom over their personal data. Every mobile device should be free from government and corporate control, and should have unrestricted access  to information on the internet. We use DAO to validate and publish apps in its app stores. Moreover, it has a native utility token that allows users in the ecosystem to monetize their data and purchase apps no matter where they come from.
